   - Collect examples of effective safety app documentation

**Deliverables:**
- User experience report (5-10 pages)

- Competitive analysis presentation (10-15 slides)

## 💡 Tips for Success

1. **Test everything yourself** - Use the app extensively before documenting
2. **Think like a beginner** - Don't assume users know technical terms
3. **Use clear language** - Avoid jargon, write in simple English
4. **Include visuals** - Screenshots and diagrams make guides easier to follow
5. **Get feedback early** - Share drafts with team regularly
6. **Stay organized** - Keep files well-structured and backed up
7. **Be empathetic** - Remember this is for sensitive topics; tone matters
8. **Ask questions** - Don't hesitate to reach out to the development team
